BMW

ABOUT US

It started with a dream and a boxer engine. In 1923, the first BMW motorcycle rolled off the line in Munich — and the world of two-wheeled travel was forever changed.

"The journey is the destination. We simply build the finest machine to take you there."

From the legendary R 32 to the cutting-edge S 1000 RR, every motorcycle we've ever built carries the same obsession: engineering that serves the rider, not the other way around.

Today, BMW Motorrad stands at the crossroads of tradition and tomorrow — crafting machines that are as connected to the road as they are to the future.

Milestones

100 Years of History

1923
The R 32 — Where It All Began

Max Friz designs the R 32, BMW's first motorcycle, unveiled at the Paris Motor Show. The boxer engine layout that would define BMW Motorrad forever.

1939
World Speed Record

Ernst Henne sets the motorcycle world speed record at 279.5 km/h on a supercharged BMW — a record that stood for 14 years.

1969
The /5 Series Revolution

BMW launches the new /5 series — a clean-sheet redesign that modernized the brand and set the tone for a new generation of riders.

2000
BMW S 1000 RR Unveiled

BMW enters the superbike segment with the S 1000 RR — a race-bred masterpiece that conquered World Superbike championships and hearts worldwide.

2023
100 Years of BMW Motorrad

The centenary celebration — over 209,000 motorcycles delivered globally, marking the most successful year in the brand's storied history.
